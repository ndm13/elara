Privacy Policy
--------------

To ensure functionality, reliability, and security, the bot logs specific operational data when you interact with it. This data may include:
- Discord handles that invoke commands.
- Command execution logs, including the names of commands run, the time of execution, and the arguments provided.
- Error logs generated during command execution.

#### Data Usage
The collected data is used exclusively for:
- Providing and maintaining the bot's core functionality.

- Monitoring for abuse, spam, and violations of the Terms of Service.

#### Data Sharing and Retention
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with operational requirements.
